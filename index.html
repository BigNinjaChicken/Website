About Me

Hey, I'm Frazier Kyle, a Computer Science major at Miami University, and I am deeply passionate about game development. My interest in creating interactive and engaging gameplay experiences led me to work as a Developer and Designer at Nitric Concepts, where I programmed interactive levels and created background systems. Additionally, I developed maps and games in collaboration with Mojang and YouTubers. As the President of Miami University's Game Design Club, I lead a team in creating a finished game while also serving as a trainer, ensuring that my team members have the necessary skills to create a fun game that meets standards. I enjoy rock climbing, creating generative digital art, and 3D modeling in my free time. These activities serve as a way for me to unwind and inspire me for my game development projects. I am currently seeking internship opportunities in game development that will help me grow as a developer and lead to future employment.
